Therapy

Here at Perspective our main focus is on Gestalt therapy. Gestalt psychology is a school of thought that looks at the human mind and behavior as a whole. When trying to make sense of the world around us, Gestalt psychology suggests that we do not simply focus on every small component. Instead, our minds tend to perceive objects as elements of more complex systems. Another type of therapy that we deal in are OH cards. OH cards are a type of metaphoric associative cards used for guidance and storyrelling.

Services

There are several services that are offered here by Perspective. Mainly therapy and counseling for individuals, therapy and counseling for couples and OH card associative therapy. The master's thesis of our head archivist was on couple councelling in regard to the dark triad personalities.
